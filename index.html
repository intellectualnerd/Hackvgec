Our Vision

Introducing HackVGEC, a unique hackathon designed exclusively for students to unleash their creativity, showcase their skills and transform their innovative ideas into reality.

At HackVGEC, we aim to empower and nurture the potential of students by providing them with a platform to explore the endless possibilities of technology and inspire them to shape a better world. The hackathon is an exciting two days event packed with challenging and innovative themes, where students can brainstorm, collaborate and develop solutions to real-world problems.

Our team of experienced mentors and industry experts will be available to guide the participants and provide support throughout the hackathon. This is an opportunity for students to take their coding skills to the next level and put their creativity to the test. We encourage all students to participate and showcase their talent. We look forward to welcoming the participants and watching them embark on a journey of innovation and discovery.
